One-Line Draw

Trace every path in a single stroke — without retracing any line!
10 progressively harder shapes • 3 lives

How to Play

  • 🖊️ Tap & drag from dot to dot to draw every line.
  • ➡️ You must finish in one continuous stroke — but you lift your finger between edges to start the next one.
  • 🚫 You cannot retrace a line (overlap costs a life).
  • 🔄 Reset button restarts the shape with no penalty.
  • 🎁 Clear 3 levels in a row without losing a life to earn a life back (max 3).
